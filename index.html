Site Intent

Signs by Design provides custom sign creation, installation, refurbishment and repair to residents and businesses in the Taylors, SC area. Signs by Design specializes in advertising and information signs. They have requested a redesign of their existing website that the owner created 10-years ago using a then, outdated website builder. They would like to have their existing information organized better as well as better showcase their past and current projects. They have also expressed interest in gaining more business through search engine optimization and the first step is creating a site that is searchable.

Client Goals

Deploy a better website that will provide better SEO metrics to increase visitors and conversion rates. Create Portfolio page to better organize images and improve page load times.

Target Audience

Desislava Nikolova

Residential

Desislava is 34-years old, married with one child, originally from Bulgaria and has a thick accent. She works on the weekends as a children's hair stylist, attends an online college for Media Communications and has not seen her family in Bulgaria in over a year. Her parents will be coming in January and she wants to have a welcome banner created for their arrival and welcoming party. However, the sign needs to be printed in a Cyrillic font and written in Bulgarian since her parents do not speak english. She plans to provide the text to the sign company of her choice after viewing their respective portfolios on her MacBook Pro and iPhone 5 and would like to conduct business by email due to her accent sometimes causing confusion.

Bryan Gill

Commercial

Bryan is 38-years old and married with one child. He in the 18th year of his career as an IT professional. He is in the process of starting a web design company called Space Coast Design Studio and is looking for a sign for his office space and advertising material. He would like to keep the sign and advertising material with one company to minimize the number of companies he needs to interact with to get the job done. Bryan currently has a full time job and attends an online college for web design. He also takes care of his family and has very little free time. He plans on reviewing information and portfolios on PCs, MacBook Pro, and iPhone 5 for three sign companies and will call to schedule an appointment to get the process started.
